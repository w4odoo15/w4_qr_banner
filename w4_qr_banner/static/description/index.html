Banner ads on Swiss QR-invoices

In Switzerland, invoices must include QR payment slips, enabling customers to settle payments swiftly and conveniently by scanning a QR code. Additionally, these payment slips present a subtle advertising opportunity. Many businesses already utilize them for placing ads. Our app extends this capability to Odoo, allowing companies to seamlessly integrate advertising banners into QR invoices.
